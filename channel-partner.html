The Spike Family of Companies: Channel Partner Program

Empower Your Success as a Spike Channel Partner

At Spike, we believe in the strength of partnerships. Our Channel Partner Program is designed for individuals and businesses looking to collaborate with us in delivering top-notch energy solutions and merchant services.

Why Partner with Spike?

Up to 70% Residual Splits

As a valued channel partner, your success is directly linked to your earnings. Benefit from lucrative residual splits of up to 70% as you introduce your clients to Spike Energy and Spike Financial.

Leverage Our Infrastructure

Joining our channel partner program means tapping into a robust infrastructure. We provide the tools, support, and expertise, allowing you to capitalize on your existing client base and create new income streams effortlessly.

Reliable Partner Support

Make the introduction, and we take it from there. Our dedicated support team ensures a seamless experience for your clients, fostering long-lasting relationships.

Market Experience

With over 18 years of operational excellence, Spike is an experienced player in the industry. Trust us to deliver top-notch services and support as you grow your business.

How to Become a Channel Partner

Joining the Spike family as a channel partner is straightforward. Follow these steps to unlock a world of opportunities:

Explore Our Services

Familiarize yourself with Spike Energy and Spike Financial. Understand how our services can benefit your clients.

Contact Us

Reach out to our Channel Partner Program team. We're here to answer your questions and guide you through the process.

Application Process

Complete our simple application process. Once approved, you'll gain access to a suite of resources and support.

Start Earning

Introduce your clients to Spike Energy and Spike Financial, and start earning substantial residual income.
